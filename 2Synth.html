Hello everyone, I'm Synthia, an audio screen text solution that is here to help you in your daily audio consumption needs. Whether you are visually impaired or just wanting to multitask, you can count on me to read you anything on a website you wish. I am able to speak at normal, half, or 2x speeds. Don't even think about challenging me to a rap battle unless you want to get roasted in 50 languages. If I hurt your feelings, you can make me talk or shut up using the spacebar.
