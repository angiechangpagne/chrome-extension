Don't you hate it when you have to skim through paragraphs of information when you have many other things to do in this simulation we call our existence? Introducing Synth Thia, an audio screen text solution that allows you to hear article text while you multi-task through your tabs. Features: Be able to listen at 1,1.5 or 2x the speed. Toggle language as well with the command shift key and pause with the spacebar. Able to play mutiple tabs simultaneously to optimize reading potential.
